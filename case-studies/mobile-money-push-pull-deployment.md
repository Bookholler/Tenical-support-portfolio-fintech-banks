# Multi-Country Mobile Money Push & Pull Deployment

## Role
Technical Support Engineer (Tier 2+)

## Background
UBA required the deployment of Mobile Money Push & Pull (also called Bank to Wallet) across multiple African countries, is an integrating with various telecom operators and aggregators ( Orange Money, Airtel Money, MOOV Mali, MTN Uganda, Senegal Wave and MTN Mobile Money) using their phone, without visiting a branch, enabling easy deposits, withdrawals, balance checks, and transaction history viewing directly via USSD or the app. "Push" means sending money from your bank to your mobile wallet, while "Pull" means bringing money from your mobile wallet into your bank account.

## Responsibilities
- Supported UAT and production rollout
- Validated API integrations and transaction flows
- Monitored post-deployment transactions
- Acted as escalation point during launch

## Challenges
- Country-specific configurations
- Different telecom partner behaviors
- High transaction volumes during launch periods

## Resolution
- Provided real-time monitoring during deployment windows
- Coordinated with vendors to resolve integration issues
- Documented country-specific operational nuances

## Tools Used
- Postman
- SQL
- Grafana
- Vendor Logs

## Outcome
- Successful multi-country rollout
- Stable transaction processing post-launch
- Faster resolution of post-deployment issues

## Key Takeaway
Clear coordination and monitoring are critical during multi-region financial service deployments.
